## Purpose

This document explains how Project Brain thinks about software, knowledge, AI and long-term maintainability.

## Philosophy

Project Brain is not designed to make software teams move faster at any cost.

It is designed to make them move with understanding.

Speed without memory creates chaos. Automation without governance creates dependency. AI without discipline creates confusion.

Project Brain exists to make complex projects explainable, maintainable and transferable.
