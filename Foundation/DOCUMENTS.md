# Project Brain Document Registry

**Document ID**: FS-002  
**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Status**: Draft  
**Owner**: Project Brain  
**Last Update**: 2026-07-03

## Purpose

This document is the official catalogue of the Project Brain knowledge base.

It allows any human or AI assistant to quickly understand which documents exist, why they exist, what their status is and how they relate to each other.

## Audience

- Product Owner
- Architects
- Developers
- AI assistants
- New collaborators
- Future maintainers

## Rules

Every official document must be listed in this registry.

Each document must have:

- a unique ID;
- a title;
- a version;
- a status;
- a purpose;
- an owner;
- related documents.

A document that is absent from this registry is considered unofficial.
