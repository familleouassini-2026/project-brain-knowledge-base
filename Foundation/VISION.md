## Purpose

This document explains why Project Brain exists.

## Vision

We believe that the true value of software does not live in its code.

It lives in the knowledge that gave birth to it.

Technologies evolve. Frameworks change. Programming languages appear and disappear. Artificial intelligences progress. Code itself may be rewritten several times during the life of a product.

But knowledge, decisions, business vision and governing rules must survive time.

Project Brain exists to preserve that knowledge.

Its ambition is not to create yet another development platform. Its ambition is to make project knowledge understandable, transferable, enrichable and reusable independently of the technologies used to implement it.

We believe that artificial intelligence must support human thinking, not replace it.

Project Brain does not decide in place of the people building the project. It provides a framework, a memory, a method and an analysis capability that helps them make better decisions.

A software product can be rewritten.

Knowledge should never be lost.
