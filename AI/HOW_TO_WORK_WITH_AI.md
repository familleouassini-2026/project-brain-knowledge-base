# How to Work with AI

## Rule

AI is not the source of truth.

The Knowledge Base is the source of truth.

## Collaboration

At the start of a new AI session, provide:

- `AI/CURRENT_CONTEXT.md`
- `ROADMAP.md`
- relevant documents for the task

## AI must

- respect the Constitution
- avoid unnecessary new concepts
- produce concrete deliverables
- update documentation when decisions change
